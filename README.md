# Healthcare Financial Performance & Budget Variance Analysis

## Business Problem
A healthcare organization needs to control departmental 
spending across 8 divisions. Leadership wants to identify 
which departments are over budget, by how much, and where 
to intervene before Q1 2025.

## What I Built
A full end-to-end Power BI dashboard analyzing 1,000 
financial records across 8 departments and 5 regions — 
from raw data to CFO-ready insights.

## Key Findings
- 🔴 Clinical Operations: 24% over budget — highest variance
- 🔴 Pharmacy: 24% over budget — combined risk area
- 🟢 HR & Staffing & Facilities: 14% UNDER budget
- 💰 Total overspend: $2.66M against $106M budget
- 📊 50.1% of all records exceeded their budget

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, NumPy) — dataset generation
- Power BI — dashboard, KPIs, variance analysis
- DAX — measures for variance %, margin %, averages

## Dashboard Sections
1. Executive KPI Cards — Total Budget, Actual Spend, 
   Variance, Avg Variance %, Avg Profit Margin %
2. Budget Variance by Department — highlights over/under
3. Budget vs Actual Spend — side by side comparison
4. Monthly Spend Trend — 24-month cost curve
5. Over vs Under Budget Distribution — donut breakdown
